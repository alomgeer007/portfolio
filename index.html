    I am a Ph.D. researcher specializing in building and improving generative AI, focusing on translation, summarization, and data-to-text generation tasks. My work integrates deep learning-based models with probabilistic controls to enhance text generation techniques. In Health Informatics, I apply these AI methods to develop patient-facing solutions, enabling a better understanding of health data, such as medical records and test results, through AI-driven explanations and summarizations. I am also passionate about open-source NLP and deep learning, contributing to projects aimed at making these systems safer, more transparent, and easier to use. My goal is to bridge the gap between advanced AI technology and healthcare, improving patient outcomes through personalized, AI-supported care.

    Tiredness Measurement

    This paper uses computer vision to detect driver tiredness in real time, helping prevent accidents. The Haar-cascade classifier identifies whether eyes are open or closed.
